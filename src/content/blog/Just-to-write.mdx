### Meet, Eat and Talk

Crisis, cutbacks, restrictions, and other obstacles to artistic work are all too familiar to us. A great deal of time and energy goes into solving the immediate, the here and now. But we also need to talk about visions—about new goals and ways of thinking beyond what is currently possible. We must inform the public discourse that shapes how people see who we are and what we do. Art as nourishing, not depleting, to the social body. Art as a prerequisite for societal and cultural development. Visions should fuel the strategies needed for growth and transformation. What could be possible?

Let’s meet, eat and talk on this and more. Welcome to Skogen December 5th at 6pm.

*Efva*

Kris, neddragningar, begränsningar och andra hinder för konstnärlig verksamhet känner vi väl till. Mycket tid och energi går till att lösa det akuta, här och nu. Konst som närande, inte tärande på samhällskroppen. Konst som förutsättning för samhällelig och kulturell utveckling.
